Extends decay timers on 33 items. Food 5x longer, other decay 3x longer. Not compatible with Hardcore Rebalance Pack.

Requires **[JimK72's Icarus Mod Manager](https://github.com/Jimk72/Icarus_Software)**

## Changes

### Food Items (5x longer)
| Item | Original | New |
|------|----------|-----|
| Raw Meat | 5 min | 25 min |
| Cooked Meat | 5 min | 25 min |
| Generic Food | 5 min | 25 min |
| Fruits/Vegetables | 5 min | 25 min |
| Dried Meat/Fish/Veg | 5 min | 25 min |
| Smoked Meat | 5 min | 25 min |
| Frozen Food | 3.3 min | 16 min |
| Coconuts | 10 min | 50 min |
| Milk/Cream | 5 min | 25 min |

### Other Items (3x longer)
| Item | Original | New |
|------|----------|-----|
| General Decay | 5 min | 15 min |
| Ice | 5 min | 15 min |
| Bait | 10 min | 30 min |
| Tree Trunks | 10 min | 30 min |
| Advanced Leather | 5 min | 15 min |
| Volatile Substance | 5 min | 15 min |
| Arrows | 60 sec | 180 sec |
| Delivery Objects | 10 sec | 30 sec |
| Quick-decay | 1 sec | 3 sec |

## Compatibility

- ⚠️ **Not compatible with Hardcore Rebalance Pack** — both modify D_Decayable in opposite directions
- ✅ Compatible with Performance Optimizer (decay extensions stack)

## Installation

Import `Extended_Spoil_Timers.EXMODZ` via Icarus Mod Manager.
