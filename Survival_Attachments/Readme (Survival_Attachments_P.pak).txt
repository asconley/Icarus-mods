Survival_Attachments_P.pak
----------------------------------------------------------------------
Mod Version: 1.0

Author: AgentKush

Compatible with Icarus: All Weeks

Mod Type: EXMOD

## Description:
Adds food, oxygen, and water armor attachments. Doubles attachment slots on every armor piece (Head/Arms/Body/Legs/Feet from 1 to 2). Three new craftable attachments at the Alteration Bench:

  - Oxygen Recycler (Head slot)     -25% oxygen consumption
  - Nutrient Dispenser (Body slot)  -25% food consumption
  - Hydration Cell (Legs slot)      -25% water consumption

Arms and Feet receive the second slot but no new attachment types - use them for existing base-game attachments (Melee Damage, Tool Use, Fall Resistance, etc.).

Ver 1.0
Initial release. Added 3 new armor attachments. Doubled StartingSlots from 1 to 2 on all 5 armor container types.

## Files Modified:
  Inventory-D_InventoryInfo       (5 entries - slot bumps)
  Alterations-D_Alterations       (3 entries - stat effects)
  Attachments-D_IcarusAttachments (3 entries - attachment defs)
  Traits-D_Itemable               (3 entries - display data)
  Items-D_ItemsStatic             (3 entries - item defs)
  Items-D_ItemTemplate            (3 entries - templates)
  Crafting-D_ProcessorRecipes     (3 entries - Alteration Bench recipes)

## Installation:
  1. Install JimK72's Icarus Mod Manager
