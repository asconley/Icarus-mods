## Features

### Mega Arrow Batches (50x) — 11 recipes
Craft 50 arrows at once at the same stations as vanilla, with 10x the materials:
- Stone, Bone (Character/Crafting Bench)
- Flint, Steel, Obsidian, Caveworm (Anvil Bench+)
- Aluminium, Carbon, Cold Steel (Anvil T3+)
- Composite, Titanium, Iron Wood (Anvil T4)

### Mega Bullet Batches (50x) — 7 recipes
- Ammo Casings, Crude Ammo
- Pistol, Rifle, Assault Rifle rounds
- Shotgun Buckshot and Slugs

### Mega Special Ammo (50x) — 24 recipes
All 4 weapon types × 6 ammo variants:
- **Explosive** — Pistol/Rifle/AR/Shotgun
- **Incendiary** — Pistol/Rifle/AR
- **Armor Piercing** — Pistol/Rifle/AR
- **Obsidian** — Pistol/Rifle/AR/Shotgun
- **Cold Steel** — Pistol/Rifle/AR/Shotgun
- **Iron Wood** — Pistol/Rifle/AR/Shotgun

### Ammo Conversion — 8 recipes
Switch between ammo types at Machining Bench/Fabricator:
- Pistol ↔ Rifle ↔ AR (20 pistol = 10 rifle = 10 AR)
- Buckshot ↔ Slug (10 buckshot = 5 slugs)

## Compatibility

- ✅ Compatible with Faster Crafting (stacks — fast AND bulk)
- ✅ Compatible with Stack Size Overhaul

## Installation

Import `Ammo_Crafting_Overhaul.EXMODZ` via Icarus Mod Manager.
