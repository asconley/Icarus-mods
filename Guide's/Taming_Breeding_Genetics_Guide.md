# Taming, Breeding & Genetics — Mod Guide

A quick guide to getting the most out of the creature mods: **Taming & Tames Overhaul** and **Genetics & Breeding Overhaul**. Both are data-only EXMODs and stack with each other and with the difficulty mods (no base combat-stat changes).

## The creature pipeline

1. **Tame** a creature → 2. **Breed** it for offspring → 3. **Engineer** the genetics over generations. The two mods cover the whole loop.

## Taming & Tames Overhaul

| What | Vanilla | With mod |
|------|---------|----------|
| Taming time | 10–30 min | ~3–10 min (3× faster) |
| Gestation | full | halved (2× faster) |
| Juvenile spawn chance | 30–75% | doubled (cap 100%) |
| Food needed to tame | full | halved |
| Tame-growth (Well-Fed) XP | 30 | 90 (3×) |
| Mount speed (Husbandry talent) | +5% | +20% |

Tip: feed juveniles often — the Well-Fed XP bonus is tripled, so they mature much faster.

## Genetics & Breeding Overhaul

Boosts the **Genetics talent tree** (Week 220 "Mendel" system) — every point you spend goes twice as far:

- **Mutation:** genotype + phenotype mutation chance doubled — faster stat-point gains and rarer cosmetics.
- **Twins:** up to +60% twin-birth chance — double offspring per pregnancy.
- **Gestation & recovery:** doubled, so you cycle breeding pairs faster.
- **Wild stock:** wild-caught creatures are likelier to start with good genes (`WildGenome` / `WildBloodline` / `WildPhenome`).

**Bloodline rarity rebalance:**
- `Wild` weighting 300 → 75 — the plain baseline (with its +5% food/water cost) shows up far less, so trait bloodlines appear more.
- `Alpha` weighting 10 → 25 — the prestige `+20% size` bloodline is rare but now actually achievable.

### Selective-breeding tips
- Spec the **Genetics talent tree** first — the mod only multiplies what the talents grant.
- Breed for **total stat points** (max 65 across the 7 genotype attributes), not a single stat — offspring inherit ~50/50.
- Chase **Alpha** and **Ambitious** (+50% XP) bloodlines; with the rebalance they show up far more often.
