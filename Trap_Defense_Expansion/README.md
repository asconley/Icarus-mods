# Trap and Defense Expansion

23 new defense recipes using real in-game items. Unlocks 4 elemental landmines that were previously enemy-only, adds cheaper early-game fortifications, alternative trap recipes without rare creature drops, and batch crafting packs.

## Features

### Early-Game Fortifications (5 recipes)
Cheaper alternatives craftable at Character level — no workbench needed:
- **Quick Wood Spikes** — 40 Wood + 20 Sticks + 10 Fiber (vanilla needs 80 Wood + Rope at Crafting Bench)
- **Quick Wood Wall** — 30 Wood + 15 Sticks + 15 Fiber
- **Quick Wood Gate** — 25 Wood + 10 Sticks + 10 Fiber
- **Quick Wood Walkway** — 15 Wood + 8 Sticks
- **Batch Wood Spikes (x3)** — 200 Wood + 12 Rope at Crafting Bench

### Snare & Animal Traps (3 recipes)
- **Primitive Snare Trap** — Character crafting (vanilla requires Animal Bench)
- **Improvised Scorpion Trap** — Anvil Bench, no Scorpion Pincers needed
- **Batch Snare Traps (x4)** — Animal Bench/Crafting Bench

### Standard Landmines (2 recipes)
- **Field Landmine** — Machining Bench with simpler materials (vanilla: Fabricator + Composites)
- **Batch Landmines (x5)** — Fabricator

### ⚡ Elemental Landmines — Previously Uncraftable! (8 recipes)
These 4 landmine variants exist in the game but had NO crafting recipes — they were enemy-only items. Now craftable two ways each:

**Upgrade Path** (base Landmine + element):
- **Poison Mine** — Landmine + 10 Spoiled Food + Volatile Substance
- **Burn Mine** — Landmine + 10 Sulfur + Volatile Substance
- **Shock Mine** — Landmine + 6 Copper Wire + 2 Electronics
- **Freeze Mine** — Landmine + 20 Ice + Volatile Substance

**Direct Craft** (no base landmine needed):
- All 4 elemental types craftable from raw materials at Fabricator

### Hedgehog Defense Traps (3 recipes)
Alternative recipes without needing rare creature drops:
- **Wolf Trap** — Steel + Electronics + Gold Wire + Leather (vanilla needs Black Wolf Fur + Teeth)
- **Mammoth Trap** — Platinum + Electronics + Titanium + Ice (vanilla needs Frozen Wool + Ice Armor)
- **Lava Mine** — Steel + Electronics + Volatile Substance + Sulfur (vanilla needs Lava Hunter drops)

### Batch Defense Packs (2 recipes)
- **Defense Spikes Pack (x4)** — Crafting/Machining Bench
- **Landmine Field Kit (x5)** — Fabricator

## Installation

Import `Trap_Defense_Expansion.EXMODZ` via Icarus Mod Manager.
