# Creature Drop Enhancer

Doubles all creature butchery and skinning outputs across 62 recipes. Every animal gives 2x leather, bone, fur, meat, and rare drops.

## Changes

### Skinning Bench (44 recipes)
All carcass processing outputs doubled:
| Creature | Vanilla | Modded |
|----------|---------|--------|
| Deer | 3 Meat, 30 Leather, 18 Fur, 15 Bone | 6 Meat, 60 Leather, 36 Fur, 30 Bone |
| Wolf | 3 Meat, 30 Leather, 6 Fur, 20 Bone | 6 Meat, 60 Leather, 12 Fur, 40 Bone |
| Boar | 3 Bacon, 40 Leather, 8 Fur, 20 Bone | 6 Bacon, 80 Leather, 16 Fur, 40 Bone |
| Scorpion | 2 Meat, 2 Pincers, 1 Tail, 30 Bone | 4 Meat, 4 Pincers, 2 Tails, 60 Bone |
| Sheep | 4 Meat, 6 Bone, 5 Soft Meat, 20 Wool | 8 Meat, 12 Bone, 10 Soft Meat, 40 Wool |

Covers all creatures: deer, wolves, bears, boars, jaguars, leopards, lions, rabbits, birds, pronghorn, zebra, scorpions, sheep, pigs, chickens, cows, dogs, cats, and more.

### Butchery Bench (18 recipes)
All meat processing outputs doubled — more raw meat from specialty cuts, more bacon from pigs, more chicken from poultry.

## Compatibility

- ✅ Compatible with all other mods
- ✅ Pairs well with Stack Size Overhaul (more drops need bigger stacks)
- ✅ Pairs well with Extended Spoil Timers (more meat, longer freshness)

## Installation

Import `Creature_Drop_Enhancer.EXMODZ` via Icarus Mod Manager.
