Armor_Attachment_Expansion_P.pak
----------------------------------------------------------------------
Mod Version: 1.0

Author: AgentKush

Compatible with Icarus: All Weeks

Mod Type: EXMOD

## Description:
Adds 25 new craftable armor attachments (5 per body part: Head / Arms / Body / Legs / Feet). All effects use base-game stat keys so they stack cleanly with talents, food buffs, and existing attachments.

Crafted at the Alteration Bench or Advanced Alteration Bench. Uniform recipe: 2 Steel Ingot + 2 Epoxy + 1 Electronics per attachment.

HEAD
  - Thermal Visor            +15% Exposure Resistance
  - Hunter's Optics          +25% chance to highlight attacked targets
  - Aero Cowl                +30% Wind Damage Resistance
  - Target Painter           +25% chance to mark attacked targets
  - Predator Mask            -15% Animal Threat Modifier

ARMS
  - Reinforced Gauntlets     +15% Melee Damage
  - Quick-Loader Rig         +25% Reload Speed
  - Striker's Grip           +10% Attack Speed
  - Hemorrhage Coating       +25% chance attacks cause Bleed
  - Artisan's Pads           +20% Crafting Speed

BODY
  - Ballistic Plate          +15% Physical Damage Resistance
  - Reactive Weave           +15% Maximum Health
  - Poison Shielding         +25% Poison Damage Resistance
  - Fire Shielding           +25% Fire Damage Resistance
  - Cargo Harness            +20 Carry Weight

LEGS
  - Sprint Servos            +10% Movement Speed
  - Ankle Braces             +30% chance to resist Sprain
  - Marathon Circuit         -20% Stamina Action Cost
  - Endurance Coil           +20% Stamina Regen
  - Slow-Res Plating         +30% Slow Resistance

FEET
  - Cave Crawlers            +15% Cave Health Regen
  - Aqua Soles               +25% Swim Speed
  - Scaler Grips             +30% Climbing Speed
  - Shock Pads               +30% Fall Damage Resistance
  - Homestead Soles          +3 Comfort

Ver 1.0
Initial release. 25 new attachments across 6 data tables.

## Files Modified:
  Alterations-D_Alterations          (25 entries - stat effects)
  Attachments-D_IcarusAttachments    (25 entries - attachment defs)
  Traits-D_Itemable                  (25 entries - display data)
  Items-D_ItemsStatic                (25 entries - item defs with armor body-part tags)
  Items-D_ItemTemplate               (25 entries - templates)
  Crafting-D_ProcessorRecipes        (25 entries - Alteration Bench recipes)

## Installation:
  1. Install JimK72's Icarus Mod Manager
     https://github.com/jimk72/IcarusModManager
  2. Download Armor_Attachment_Expansion.EXMODZ
  3. Import via Mod Manager

## Pairs Well With:
  - Survival_Attachments (doubles attachment slots so you can run more at once)
