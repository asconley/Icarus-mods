# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a welcoming and respectful experience for everyone, regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

## Our Standards

**Expected behavior:**

- Be respectful and constructive in all interactions
- Give and accept feedback gracefully
- Focus on what's best for the community
- Show empathy towards other community members
- Help newcomers get started

**Unacceptable behavior:**

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments
- Trolling or deliberately inflammatory remarks
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Spam, self-promotion, or off-topic content in issues/discussions
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces — GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, Discord, and any other community channels associated with this project.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening an issue or contacting the maintainer directly. All reports will be reviewed and addressed appropriately. Responses may include warnings, temporary bans, or permanent removal from the community.
