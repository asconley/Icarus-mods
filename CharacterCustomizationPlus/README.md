# CharacterCustomizationPlus

Expands Icarus character creation with unlocked content, more eye colors, suit colors, cap/hair colors, and a huge palette of tattoo/accent colors.

## Features

### Unlocked Content (3)
- Spacesuit Male & Female (normally hidden)
- Deluxe Edition suit color (no DLC required, UI swatch fixed)

### Eye Colors (+25 new, 34 total)
Greens (Emerald, Forest, Jade, Lime), Warm tones (Amber, Honey, Copper, Gold), Purples (Violet, Amethyst, Lavender, Rose), Blues (Ice Blue, Electric Blue, Sapphire, Teal, Cyan), Greys (Steel, Silver, Charcoal), Exotics (Crimson, Ruby, Ember, Molten, White)

### Suit Colors (+6 new, 16 total)
Arctic White, Midnight, Forest Green, Rust, Slate Blue, Sand — all with proper 4-color UI swatches and full MaterialOverrides

### Tattoo/Accent Colors (+18 new, 33 total)
Neons (Cyan, Magenta, Yellow, Orange, Lime), Earth Tones (Olive, Terracotta, Slate, Burnt Sienna), Deep/Rich (Purple, Blood Red, Midnight Blue, Forest), Metallics/Light (Pale Gold, Bronze, Ice White, Ash Grey, Rose Pink)

### Cap/Hair Colors (+6 new, 8 total)
Platinum Blonde, Auburn, Copper Red, Silver Grey, Dark Brown, Navy & White (two-tone)

## Installation
Drop the `.EXMODZ` file into your Icarus Mod Manager mods folder.

## Compatibility
Modifies `D_CharacterCreationData.json` only. Compatible with most other mods.
