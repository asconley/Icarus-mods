Hidden_Building_Pieces_P.pak
----------------------------------------------------------------------
Mod Version: 4.0
Author: AgentKush
Compatible with Icarus: All Weeks
Mod Type: EXMOD

## Description:
Unlocks 62 hidden building pieces as individual items. Includes Wood diagonal/curved pieces, frames for 11 materials, corner stairs for 13 materials, glass stairs, and more. All items are craftable at material-appropriate benches.
