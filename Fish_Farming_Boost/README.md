# Fish and Farming Boost

**Author:** AgentKush | **Version:** 1.1

390 changes across farming and fishing systems. Crops grow twice as fast, harvests yield 50% more, fish spawn rates doubled, and fish quality increased.

## Changes

### Faster Crop Growth (211 stages)
All crop growth stage timers halved. Examples:
| Crop | Vanilla Total | Modded Total |
|------|--------------|-------------|
| Wheat/Beans/Berries | 90 min | 45 min |
| Corn/Pumpkin/Squash | 180 min | 90 min |
| Carrot/Coffee/Cocoa | 120 min | 60 min |
| Avocado/Coconut | 160 min | 80 min |
| Banana/Kiwi | 212 min | 106 min |
| Agave/Garlic/Onion/PricklyPear (DLC) | Varies | 50% faster |

### Better Harvest Yields (120 reward tables)
+50% min and max stack counts on all crop rewards. Get more produce per harvest.

### More Cultivations (2 farmable plots)
+2 cultivations on all plot types — get more harvests before replanting.

### Fish Spawn Boost (57 zones)
Doubled spawn weights across all 57 fish zones on every map. More fish in every body of water.

### Fish Quality Boost (55 zones)
+50% fish quality across all zones (capped at 1.0). Bigger, better fish.

## Compatibility

- ✅ Compatible with all other mods in the collection
- ✅ Stacks with Extended Spoil Timers (grow faster, lasts longer)

## Installation

Import `Fish_Farming_Boost.EXMODZ` via Icarus Mod Manager.
